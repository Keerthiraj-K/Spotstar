Merry Christmas

(2024)

      Merry Christmas is a 2024 Indian neo-noir mystery thriller film directed by Sriram Raghavan and starring Vijay Sethupathi and Katrina Kaif. The film is produced by Ramesh Taurani, Jaya Taurani, Sanjay Routray and Kewal Garg under Tips Films and Matchbox Pictures. It is based on Frédéric Dard's French novel Le Monte-charge . The 1962 French movie Paris Pick-Up was also based on the same novel.
