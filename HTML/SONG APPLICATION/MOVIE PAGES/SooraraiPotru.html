Soorarai Pottru

(2020)

      Soorarai Pottru is a 2020 Tamil action movie. This movie is directed by female director Sudha Kongara . The movie is produced by Suriya . Suriya and Aparna Balamurali played the lead roles in the film. Urvashi , Mohan Babu and Karunas are in supporting roles. The story of the film revolves around the founder of Air Deccan Airlines, Mr. Ra. Adapted from the story of Gopinath . On 12 November 2020, the film was released on the Amazon Prime Video website.
