Tamannah Movies

Tamanna Bhatia is an Indian actress who primarily works in Telugu, Tamil and Hindi cinema. She has appeared in over 80 films, and has won a Kalaimamani Award, a SIIMA Award and has received several Filmfare Awards South nominations. Tamannaah began her acting career with the Hindi film Chand Sa Roshan Chehra (2005). She then debuted in Telugu cinema with Sri (2005) and Tamil cinema with Kedi (2006).
