Shankar Movies

Shankar Shanmugam, credited as S. Shankar or his mononym Shankar, is an Indian filmmaker who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. He is one of the most prolific and highest paid film directors in India. His films typically deal with the contemporary social issues, vigilante themes and the usage of state of the art technology and VFX. He has won one National Film Award, four Filmfare Award South and six Tamil Nadu State Film Awards in his career.
